Core Features & Benefits

Smart Resume Optimization

AI-driven analysis identifies areas for improvement, suggests powerful action verbs, and ensures your resume is tailored for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Job Matching AI

Upload a job description and let our AI highlight key skills and keywords, helping you customize your resume for specific roles and increase your match rate.

Intuitive Resume Builder

Craft professional resumes from scratch or refine existing ones with our step-by-step builder, featuring multiple templates and AI-powered content suggestions for each section.

AI Cover Letter Generator

Generate persuasive cover letters tailored to specific job applications. Our AI helps you highlight your strengths and match your experience to the job requirements effectively.

AI-Powered Translation

Break language barriers. Translate your resume and cover letter into multiple languages to apply for international job opportunities seamlessly.

ATS Compatibility Check

Get detailed feedback on how well your resume will parse through common Applicant Tracking Systems, ensuring it reaches human eyes.
